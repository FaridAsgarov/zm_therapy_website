Services

Individual Psychotherapy/Psychoanalysis

My approach is that "every human is a new world". I was raised in the psychoanalytic method (Freudian and Winnicottian). I believe in the 'talking cure'. Therefore, I support individuals living their truth by making the unconscious conscious. However, we humans are rather tricky creatures and only change if and when we want to. Furthermore, 'to change' is not only overcoming the traumas, repressed memories, and everything scary that lays in your mental Pandora's box but also accepting the traits of your personality that you perceive as good and bad and becoming a healthier version of yourself with the worked through new knowledge. To adjust to change healthily, an individual must master self-awareness which we will cultivate in you by practicing, for example, the psychoanalytic technique of free association, dream analysis, and other psychotherapeutic techniques.

Couple Psychotherapy/Psychodynamic Psychotherapy

I mostly work with couples who are having relationship issues. We will work through balancing your expectations as a couple, improve your communication with your partner, and enhance your physical, emotional, and spiritual intimacy with your partner. We will discuss in detail your anxieties, yearnings, and attachment styles to offer you both a holding environment in your relationship and make you understand each other better. In the matter of divorce, I also offer support to you as a couple through the traumatic procedure and help you separate as healthily as possible. Additionally, I work with couples who are, for example, 'new parents', share the trauma of the loss of a child, or want to rekindle the flame of their relationship after multiple years of being together.

Coaching

I believe there is no such thing as 'too much' empowerment. My goal for my clients is to become more successful in their life and unlock the door of self-actualization. I accomplish that by locating an individual's balance of work and pleasure, demolishing an individual's triggers, and growing their ego strength, thereby elevating an individual's self-reliance.
