Welcome to my website!

My name is Zoya Mesaric. I'm a Psychotherapist and Psychoanalyst (i.A.u.S.) with 6+ years of working experience. I am based in Vienna, Austria. I speak Slovenian, Serbo-Croatian, English fluently, and Spanish.

My interest in psychotherapy, especially psychoanalysis, blossomed very early. As Friedrich Nietzsche said, "he who has a why to live can bear almost any how," and I was a curious child in the constant search of the 'why'. I was interested and still am in why people behave as they do.

My main expertise is female sexuality, and my specialization is trauma. During my education, I accomplished multiple internships. Furthermore, I worked with people suffering from different mental illnesses, from those suffering from addictions to those who committed crimes such as murders.

Since I believe that 'every human is a new world', everyone requires a different treatment. I am not a fan of diagnoses; my goal is to reduce, if not eliminate, your symptoms with your active participation.

My Areas of Expertise

Anxiety

Symptoms: excessive and uncontrollable worrying, intrusive negative thoughts, agitation, restlessness, insomnia, irritability, muscle tension, breathing issues, nausea, headaches, fears and phobias.

Attachment

Symptoms: pattern repetitions, trust issues, co/dependency issues, boundary regulation issues, anger issues, control issues, abandonment issues, fear of intimacy, obsessive behavior.

Depression

Symptoms: persistent low mood, loss interest and capability to perform daily activities, low self-esteem, sleeping problems, over-eating or under-eating, suicidal ideation, loss of sexual drive.

Identity Crisis

Symptoms: constant questioning of your identity and purpose in life, gender identity crisis, mood swings and irritability, changes in sleep, appetite and weight, bullying trauma, loneliness, substance abuse.

Personality disorders

Symptoms: persistent impulsive and unstable behaviour, self-harm, inconsistent values, difficulty with empathy, difficulty regulating emotions, feelings of superiority, odd thinking, eccentric behaviour, dramatic and erratic behaviour.

Relationships

Symptoms: difficulty balancing individual and couple expectations, communication issues, infidelity, divorce, separation, financial issues, sex and intimacy issues, jealousy issues, children issues.

Sexuality

Symptoms: recovery from sexual abuse or assault, conflicting sexual desires, sex addiction, lack of sexual desire, body image issues, pain during intercourse, sexual identity crisis, fetishism, impotence, pornography addiction.

Trauma

Symptoms: uncontrollable thoughts about the event, feeling disconnected from reality, severe anxiety, feelings of guilt and shame, self-blame, difficulty concentrating, confusion, shock, denial, withdrawal from others, flashbacks, nightmares.
